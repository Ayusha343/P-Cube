# Hardware Components List

This document lists all the hardware components and items used in the P Cube – Power Pedal Pro project, based on the bill of materials.

## Core Electrical Components

- **DC Motor**: 250W 24V
- **Battery**: 24V 12Ah (sealed lead-acid)
- **Speed Controller (ESC)**: 24V 33A
- **Charger**: 14V 2A
- **Keylock Switch**: 12V 1A
- **Throttle**: 23mm 1.56M
- **Voltmeter**: DC 0-100V
- **Voltmeter and Ammeter**: 0-100V 50A
- **DPDT Switch**: 10A 220V On-Off-On + 6A On-Off-On
- **Push Button Switch**: Self lock
- **VTR Switch**: 6 pin on/off
- **USB Converter**: 12V to 5V 3A

## Mechanical Components

- **Sprocket**: 80T OD(165) ID(54)
- **Freewheel**: 22T OD(93) ID(34)
- **Chain**: 25H (main) and 1.5 feet (additional)
- **Double Sided Threaded Hub**: 34mm
- **Motor Plate**: 14cm x 8cm
- **Battery Plate**: 1.5 ft 1 inch
- **Spokes**: For 26" wheel
- **Balls**: For axle (2 × 18)
- **Lock**: 5 link
- **Philips Connector and Plug**: Cable with AC connector

## Wiring and Connectors

- **Wire**: 14 AWG
- **Wire**: 22 AWG
- **Motor and Battery Socket (Male) x 2**: XT30-U
- **Motor and Battery Socket (Female) x 2**: XT30-U
- **Connectors x 10**: 2 pins JST XH 2.54mm Pitch
- **Charger Connector**: 2.1mm, 5.5mm
- **Tactile Switch**: 12mm x 12mm x 6mm

## Enclosure and Mounting

- **Acrylic Sheet**: 4' x 2' + 2' x 3"
- **Foam Gasket**: 10mm x 2mm x 10m
- **Nut and Bolt and Washer**: M6 2"
- **Nuts and Bolts and Washers**: 4 inch × 12

## Lighting and Safety

- **Headlight**: 12V 1.2A

## Tools and Supplies

- **Heat Shrink**: 1mm 5m, 2mm 3m, 5mm 1m
- **Solder Wire**: 1mm 50g

## Fabrication Services

- **Lathe**: Sprocket lathe and freewheel weld with sprocket
- **Drill**: Motor plate holes
- **Welding**: Sprocket welded to hub
- **Chain Joining**: Chain joined and wheel alignment
- **Lacing**: Spokes joined to the wheel
- **Tube**: Valve tube replaced
