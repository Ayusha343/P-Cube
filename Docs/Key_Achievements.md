## 14.3 Impact and Significance

These recognitions reflect the project's potential to address real-world challenges in affordable transportation. The combination of technical innovation, sustainability focus, and market relevance has resonated with judges, media, and industry professionals. The project demonstrates that effective engineering solutions need not be expensive or complex. By focusing on practical problem-solving and resourceful component sourcing, accessible technology becomes achievable.
