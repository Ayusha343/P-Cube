## 12.3 Cost Advantages of the P Cube Approach

- **Recycled Materials**: UPS batteries (₹0–₹500), truck lights (₹0–₹50), timing chains (₹0– ₹100)
- **Component Sourcing**: Extensive market research identified the most cost-effective vendors
- **DIY Assembly**: Labor cost reduced to zero through personal hands-on work
- **Simplified Design**: Avoided expensive BMS, regenerative braking, or advanced sensors
- **Smart Substitutions**: Plug sockets instead of expensive DPDT switches; epoxy instead of welding where appropriate

## 12.4 Scalability and Manufacturing Costs

If manufactured at scale (100+ units), several cost reductions would be possible:

- Bulk component purchasing: 15–25% cost reduction
- Standardized manufacturing process: Labor costs reduced
- Supplier partnerships: Direct relationships with recycled material sources
- Jig and fixture investment: Improves assembly consistency and speed

**Projected Production Cost**: At scale, the P Cube could potentially be produced at ₹5,500– ₹6,500, allowing for reasonable profit margins while maintaining affordability.
