# Problem Statement

In India, daily commuting remains a significant challenge for millions, particularly for students, daily wage workers, farmers, and security personnel. The inability to afford conventional motorized transportation—such as motorcycles or scooters—creates a transportation bottleneck for those with limited financial resources.

Traditional bicycles serve as the primary mode of personal transportation for many in this demographic. However, limitations in range, speed, and physical effort required create barriers to their wider adoption, especially for longer commutes or challenging terrain.

Electric bicycles present an attractive solution, combining affordability with efficiency and sustainability. However, the existing market exhibits two problematic extremes:

- Cheap conversion kits (₹8,000–₹15,000) lack essential components, creating hidden costs for batteries, chargers, and covers
- Fully assembled electric bicycles cost ₹30,000+, placing them beyond the financial reach of target users
- Available conversion kits often exclude weather-protective elements, reducing durability in India's varied climate
- Many users lack the knowledge or resources to assemble kits from scattered components

The P Cube – Power Pedal Pro addresses this market gap by providing a comprehensive, affordable conversion kit that includes all necessary components, is made from recycled materials, and offers complete functionality at a price point (₹7,000) below most competitors while maintaining superior feature inclusion.
