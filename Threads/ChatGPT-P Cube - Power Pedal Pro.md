## Response:
The plastic disc behind the gears on a geared bicycle’s rear wheel is called a **spoke protector** or **dork disc**. Its main purpose is to prevent the chain from slipping off the largest rear cog (gear) into the spokes, which could cause damage to the wheel and potentially lead to an accident.
